City Government of Biñan

The City Government of Biñan is the local governing body responsible for delivering efficient, transparent, and citizen-centered public services. Through the adoption of modern technologies and digital solutions, the City continues to improve government operations, streamline service delivery, and strengthen engagement with the community.

Information and Communications Technology Office (ICTO)

The Information and Communications Technology Office serves as the City Government of Biñan’s primary department for digital transformation and technology-driven initiatives. It supports local government offices through systems development, technical assistance, infrastructure management, software maintenance, cybersecurity support, and other ICT services that improve public service delivery.

Internship Placement

During my practicum, I was assigned to the Application Development and Creative Support Team. My placement involved software development, UI/UX design, multimedia production, software testing, technical documentation, and collaboration on government and educational technology projects.

Practicum Objectives

The internship provided an opportunity to apply academic knowledge in a professional environment, gain experience with real-world development workflows, and strengthen the technical and interpersonal skills expected of an Information Technology practitioner.
