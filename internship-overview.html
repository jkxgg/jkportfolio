Internship Overview

My practicum at the City Government of Biñan’s Information and Communications Technology Office provided hands-on experience in software development, UI/UX design, multimedia production, system testing, technical documentation, and collaborative project work. As part of the Application Development & Creative Support Team, I contributed to government information systems, educational technology projects, and public information initiatives.

Host Organization

City Government of Biñan

The City Government of Biñan is the local governing body responsible for delivering efficient, transparent, and citizen-centered public services. Through the adoption of modern technologies and digital solutions, the City continues to improve government operations, streamline service delivery, and strengthen engagement with the community.

Assigned Department

Information and Communications Technology Office

The Information and Communications Technology Office leads the City Government of Biñan’s digital transformation and technology-driven initiatives. It supports local government departments through information systems development, technical assistance, infrastructure management, software maintenance, cybersecurity support, and other ICT services that improve public service delivery.

Team Placement

Application Development & Creative Support Team

I was assigned to a multidisciplinary team responsible for developing software solutions and producing creative digital materials for government and educational projects. My placement involved coding, interface design, multimedia asset creation, system testing, documentation, and collaborative project work.
